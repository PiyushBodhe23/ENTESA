About ENTESA

ENTESA is the premier electronics club at our college, dedicated to pushing the boundaries of innovation in embedded systems, IoT, robotics, and communication technologies.

Our mission is to create a vibrant community of makers, tinkerers, and engineers who collaborate on cutting-edge projects and participate in national competitions.

Founded in 2010, ENTESA stands for innovation and collaboration at RSCOE.

Mission

1. To entrust the students with fundamentals of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering for successful career.

2. To enable students to pursue higher education, research and promote Entrepreneurship.

3. To serve the nation through techno-social development.

Vision

"To create an educational environment to meet the challenges of modern Electronics and Telecommunication engineering industry through state of art technical knowledge and innovative approach."
